A single character representing a field in a date.

<pre class="syntax">
'G' | 'y' | 'M' | 'd' | 'a' | 'B' | 'H' | 'h' | 'm' | 's'
</pre>

### Values

- `"G"`
  - Era
- `"y"`
  - Year
- `"M"`
  - Month
- `"d"`
  - Day
- `"a"`
  - Day period (AM, PM)
- `"B"`
  - Day period flexible (e.g. "in the afternoon")
- `"H"`
  - Hour (24-hour cycle)
- `"h"`
  - Hour (12-hour cycle)
- `"m"`
  - Minute
- `"s"`
  - Second
